Breeds Of Your Pet.

Siamese cat

The International Cat Association describes the modern Siamese as affectionate, social, intelligent, and playful into adulthood, often enjoying a game of fetch and seek human interaction and also like companionship from other cats.

Persian cat

The Persian cat is a long-haired breed of cat characterized by its round face and short muzzle.The exact history of the Persian cat does seem to be a bit of a mystery but many of them are lives in hieroglyphics.

Egyptian Mau

Egyptian Maus are a small- to medium-sized short-haired cat breed.they are one of the few naturally spotted breeds of pet cat.The spots of the Mau occur on only the tips of the hairs of its coat. It is considered to be a rare breed.

American Shorthair

The American Shorthair (ASH) is a breed of domestic cat believed to be descended from European cats brought by early settlers to protect valuable cargo from mice and rats.
